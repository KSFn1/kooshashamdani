About Us

Our world today is undeniably digital. New technologies, from social media and GPS
systems to artificial intelligence and digital twins, make the planet we inhabit unrecognizable
from even 20 years ago. If you're feeling queasy at the speed of change, hold tight. It's only
going to get faster and therefore, I believe that having some sort of a digital marketing place is
beneficial.

My name is Koosha Shamdani and I’m a fourteen year old who loves computers. Since I was
a little kid, I always enjoyed learning about computers in general. Whether it was messing
around with apps, testing different elements, or playing video games, I was always trying to find
a way to learn a new thing about computers.

I live in Toronto, Canada and I can communicate in three different languages: English, French,
and Farsi.

I was first introduced to video editing by a friend of mine in 2020. My first projects were
mainly focused on video gaming content or, Fortnite montages to be precise. I use DaVinci
Resolve 17 as my video editing software and I've been using it since day one. I edited and
sold a few montages to clients that I found in discord servers. As I edited more and more
videos, I tried learning to edit other content as well. In January 2021, I started making short
advertising videos for fun. Although I have never published an advertising video, I
understand all the constituents that go into producing an effective video and I'm capable of
manufacturing it.

In October 2021, I started learning to build and maintain websites using JavaScript (HTML and
CSS). My first few projects were mainly basic, since I was focused on experiencing and learning how
things work rather than copying from a YouTube video. However, over time as I experienced more and more,
I’m now able to work with HTML and CSS to build and maintain websites that are written
in JavaScript. Overall, I have created seven functioning websites since I started and this current
website is one of them.
